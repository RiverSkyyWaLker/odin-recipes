Baked Ziti

Description

Ziti is a classic Italian pasta dish that combines ziti pasta with a rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and savory herbs. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family gatherings or a cozy dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 6 ounces provolone cheese, sliced
  • 1 ½ cups sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add ziti pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Meanwhile, brown ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat; stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the cooked ziti pasta to the sauce and mix well to coat the pasta evenly.
  7. Spread 1/2 of the ziti in the bottom of the prepared dish; top with Provolone cheese, sour cream, 1/2 of the meat sauce, remaining ziti, mozzarella cheese, and remaining meat sauce. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
  8. Layer the remaining ziti mixture on top and sprinkle with the remaining cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  10. Remove from the oven, garnish with chopped fresh basil, and serve hot.
